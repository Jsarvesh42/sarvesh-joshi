About

Sarvesh Joshi is a dedicated and professionally trained Tabla artist, performer, and educator. His musical journey began under the tutelage of Late Shri Subhash K. Kampoowale (Rajhans) at Gandharva Sangeet Mahavidyalay, Nagpur, where he trained from 2011 to 2021. He later continued his learning under Pt. Pundalik Krushna Bhagwat (Banaras) in 2021, and presently studies with Pt. Ravindra Yavagal (Bengaluru) since 2023.

Academically, Sarvesh holds a Tabla Visharad degree from the Akhil Bhartiya Gandharva Mahavidyalay Mandal, Mumbai (ABGMVM). He earned a Gold Medal in his MA from Kavikulguru Kalidas Sanskrit University, Ramtek (KKSU), and also holds a Diploma in Journalism and Mass Communication.

His training has been enriched through guidance from Pt. Ulhas Rajhans (A-graded artist from Indore), and by participating in masterclasses and workshops conducted by legendary percussionists including Pt. Ramdas Palsule, Pt. Nayan Ghosh, and Pt. Yogesh Samsi. He was also awarded the prestigious SPIC MACAY scholarship to train under Pt. Ravindra Yavagal.

Sarvesh currently teaches Tabla at the Spardha School of Music. He regularly performs solo and accompanies vocal, instrumental, and Kathak dance recitals. He is pursuing his MA in Music (Percussion - Tabla) at the School of Performing Arts, Bharati Vidyapeeth Deemed University, Pune.
