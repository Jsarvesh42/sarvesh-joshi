About

Sarvesh Joshi is a dedicated and professionally trained Tabla artist, performer, and educator. His musical journey began at the early age of six. Later, he began his formal training under the tutelage of Late Shri. Subhash K. Kampoowale (Rajhans) at Gandharva Sangeet Mahavidyalay, Nagpur, where he trained from 2011 to 2021. He later continued his learning under Pt. Pundalik Krushna Bhagwat (Banaras) in 2021, and presently learning under the guidance of Pt. Ravindra Yavagal (Bengaluru) since 2023.

Academically, Sarvesh holds a Tabla Visharad degree from the Akhil Bhartiya Gandharva Mahavidyalay Mandal, Mumbai (ABGMVM). He earned a Gold Medal in his MA from Kavikulguru Kalidas Sanskrit University, Ramtek (KKSU), and also holds a Diploma in Journalism and Mass Communication.

His training has been enriched through guidance from Pt. Ulhas Rajhans (A-graded artist from Indore), and by participating in masterclasses and workshops conducted by legendary Tabla Artists, including Pt. Ramdas Palsule, Pt. Nayan Ghosh, and Pt. Yogesh Samsi. He is also a recepant of the SPIC MACAY Gurukul Anubhav scholarship to train under Pt. Ravindra Yavagal.

After working with some leading music institutions as a Tabla trainer, in the year 2022, Sarvesh founded "The Tabla Academy" and is currently training students around the globe through online and offline modes. He regularly performs solo and accompanies vocal, instrumental, and Kathak dance recitals. He is pursuing his MA in Music (Percussion - Tabla) at the School of Performing Arts, Bharati Vidyapeeth Deemed University, Pune.

Apart from his work as a performer and educator, Sarvesh Joshi is also a passionate researcher in the field of Indian Classical Music. He has published research papers and articles on Indian classical music as well as Sanskrit and Vedic Literature. Many of his works have been recognized and awarded at various academic and cultural forums and conferences.
